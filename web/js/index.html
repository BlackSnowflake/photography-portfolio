About Black Snowflake

Black Snowflake photography began as a hobby and really still is more of a hobby than anything else. I love to take photos. It probably has something to do with my material possessive nature. A photo is like owning a moment of time, and i like to collect stuff.

I'll be the first to tell you that photography is hard work. Even with todays latest technology capturing a truly captivating photo is a special thing. That's why I like to share my photos just in case there is one in my collection that captivates you.

Feel free to download my photos if you see something you like. Contact me for high res prints too. Thanks for visiting my world in photography.
